Privacy Policy

Utility Meter Reading ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect your name, email address, and other information you provide during registration or use of the app.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, such as meter readings, device information, and log data.
  • Device Permissions: The app may request access to Bluetooth, storage, and location to enable core features. These permissions are only used for their intended purpose.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To communicate with you about your account or support requests
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to provide core app functionality (e.g., connecting to your utility provider).

Data Security

  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

  • Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Account Deletion Policy

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact our support team. We will process your request and permanently delete your account data from our systems.

To request account deletion, email:
admin@meritadvisory.so

Please include your registered email address and any relevant details to help us identify your account. We will confirm your request and notify you once your data has been deleted.
